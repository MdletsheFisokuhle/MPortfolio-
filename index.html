The proliferation of smart devices, social media platforms, and data collection technologies has created unprecedented opportunities for surveillance and privacy violations.

The constant connectivity and information overload can result in decreased attention spans, sleep disorders, and difficulty forming real-world relationships.

Automation and AI are replacing human workers across various industries, potentially leading to widespread unemployment and increased economic inequality.
